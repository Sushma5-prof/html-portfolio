The Best K-Series

According to Sushma

Tale of the Nine-Tailed

This is one my favorite k-drama. It is fantasy genre k-drama. The storyline and the characters,
their acting were so good but a bit of a sad ending which made me a bit angry.

Goblin: The great and lonely God

This is my all time favorite k-drama. The plot was so good that we were so attached to it till the end,
and The Character have done a great job at acting and in making the viewers feel happy, emotional, entertained and many more.

Doom at your Service

This is also my favorite k-drama. It is also a fantasy drama.
The plot were also my favorites. I loved their chemistry and the storyline.
